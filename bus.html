Details

Lines 16 and 17 merge to become the new Line 16, which operates via Line 16/17 normal route between downtown LA and 3rd St/San Vicente via 3rd St, then north on San Vicente to Santa Monica Bl.

Line 16 west of 3rd St/San Vicente on Burton Way in Beverly Hills is replaced by new Line 617, which operates from E Line (Expo) Culver City Station to Cedars-Sinai Medical Center/Beverly Center via Robertson Bl, then west on Burton Way and Beverly Dr to Pico Bl. This includes new weekend service for Robertson Bl. Bus stop consolidation.
